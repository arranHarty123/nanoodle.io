NANOODLE

NANOODLE is a service for the Nano community. It aims to provide features that will fulfill the needs of users and organisations who aim to utliise Nano.

It's primary focus is on the business adoption of Nano.


Feeless. Instant. Green.

Nano is a digital currency being developed by the Nano Foundation and its open source contributers. This community is building a feeless, instant, global currency over a secure, scalable, energy efficient and decentralised network.

Nano's architecture is built around the Block-Lattice (a form of DAG), in which each account has its own blockchain. When a transaction occurs on the Block-Lattice, a block is added onto the sender and receiver's blockchains; other account's blockchains are not effected. This approach makes Nano extremely fast and scalable.

The vision for Nano is that it becomes one of the world's most popular currencies.
